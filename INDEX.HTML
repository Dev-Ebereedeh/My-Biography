Can I get to Know You?

Before anything else, I need you to know that beyond any labels, I am human. Yea just as complicated as you are. Hmmm...Let's get to the simple part. Incase you've forgotten, my name is Eberechukwu, and if you are wondering what that means, ooh well it means "God's Mercy". Fun fact, I never really liked my name growing up, I just couldn't understand why my parents resorted to such an uncute name, I mean these two adults had a whole nine months to come up with some cute name like Michelle, Lucy, you know what I mean. However, I made peace with my name, after a friend taught me this "a name doesn't make a man, rather a man makes the name". Ooh well, I will love to hear your thoughts on this...

Personal Values
